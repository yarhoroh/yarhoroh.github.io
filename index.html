How it works

1

Place your cursor

Click where you want the text - document, chat, email, anywhere.

2

Hold the hotkey

Press and hold Ctrl+Win+Alt and speak.

3

Release

Text will automatically appear at cursor position.

Tip: Press Ctrl+Win+Shift to copy last transcription to clipboard.
