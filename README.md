## About

Murmur is a voice-to-text application for Windows powered by OpenAI Whisper AI. Press and hold the hotkey, speak, release — text appears right where your cursor is. No switching apps, no copy-paste.

Works everywhere: Chrome, Word, Slack, VS Code, any application. 100% private — all processing happens locally on your device, your voice never leaves your computer.

## How It Works

1. **Place your cursor** — Click where you want the text: document, chat, email, anywhere
2. **Hold the hotkey** — Press and hold `Ctrl` + `Win` + `Alt` and speak
3. **Release** — Text will automatically appear at cursor position

> **Tip:** Press `Ctrl` + `Win` + `Shift` to copy last transcription to clipboard.
